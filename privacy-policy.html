Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

This app itself does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or account credentials. All lyrics data you create is processed and stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

However, this app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements and Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. These services automatically collect the following data:

  • Advertising ID – A device identifier used to serve personalized ads. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • App interaction data – Information such as app launches, screen views, feature usage, and tap events, used for ad delivery and analytics.
  • Diagnostic data – App performance information such as crash logs, launch time, and energy usage, used to improve ad quality and stability.

This data is collected and transmitted to Google servers encrypted via TLS. For details on how Google handles this data, see the Google Privacy Policy.

2. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – Used to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect and share data as described above for the purposes of advertising, marketing, and analytics. Users who purchase the "Save & Load" feature will no longer see banner ads, and ad-related data collection will cease.
  • Firebase Analytics – Used to collect anonymous usage data such as app opens, screen views, and feature usage. This data helps improve the app experience and is sent to Google servers. No personally identifiable information is collected through Firebase Analytics.

No other third-party analytics or advertising services are used at this time.

3. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store payment systems. Payment information is managed directly by each platform, and the developer of this app does not have access to your payment details.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

This app does not require account creation or login. Since no personal data is stored on our servers, there is no user data to delete. The advertising ID can be reset by the user at any time through the device's system settings.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
