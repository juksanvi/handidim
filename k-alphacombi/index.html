A Note About This App

AlphaCombi maps Korean letters to alphabet letters based on the Revised Romanization of Korean (RR), South Korea's official romanization standard. It is NOT a phonetic pronunciation guide — Korean has sound-change rules (linking, nasalization, etc.) that are not reflected here, because showing them would obscure the letter-assembly structure. Think of it as a cheat sheet for reading along, not a Korean lesson.
